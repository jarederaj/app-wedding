We’re getting married on Sunday, August 17, 2014 at 2:30 PM MST.

We like to think of this as simply a church service and a party. The church service is at Christ Our Hope Anglican Church in Fort Collins, and the party is in Mark and Emily Maeda’s front yard in Mead, Colorado.

Be aware that our wedding is the same weekend as Bohemian Nights at NewWestFest! So, plan for that.

Service

Attire: Casual Sunday Dress

We’ll say “I will” at Christ Our Hope Anglican Church with beautiful music and ancient liturgy. Much like any ordinary Sunday at our church, this traditional Anglican service will have two parts: the liturgy of the word (where we say our vows, have some readings and prayers) and the liturgy of the table (Eucharist or communion service).

Party

Our friends, Mark and Emily Maeda, generously offered their front yard for a reception after the ceremony. We’ll have plenty of food and beer in a happy place with our friends and family. Kid and adult–friendly. Wear sensible shoes or kick your shoes off.

Go to the Maeda’s house in Mead, about 30 minutes south of Fort Collins.
