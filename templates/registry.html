Jared doesn’t like fancy internet things but didn’t bother to build something simple instead. (It would be nice if you went to that page and saw all the clever things there, but it’s a shell of its former glory.)

Laura encouraged Jared to use one fancy internet tool, myregistry.com. We registered for items at various stores, and items not available at The Kitchenette are available in one list at myregistry.com. If you want to do this in the most painless way possible, just go there. Or, view the list of stores where we have registered for items.

We’re registered for many of our kitchen items at The Kitchenette in Price Hardware in Atlanta, Texas. Items can be shipped easily.

Our complete list of registries is below.

Artists and artwork

There’s a backstory here: friends of ours recently got married and apparently received artwork that they hadn’t asked for and didn’t want. If you choose to give art, we would like to a) support local artists and b) get pieces that we love.

Please be sensitive to the schedules and concerns of artists. We do not need a lot of art and do not have many walls to put it on.
